Who am I?

Hi I'm Lilian Weng (翁荔)! Currently I'm working in the Robotics team @ OpenAI. Before that I worked for Affirm on the credit underwriting model and Dropbox on various projects related to data science and production engineering.

I was a Ph.D. student in Complex Networks and Systems, IUB and a member of CNetS, where I closely collaborated with Prof. Filippo Menczer, Alessandro Flammini, and Yong-Yeol Ahn on research projects on information diffusion, social contagion, and modeling.
