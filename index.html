Who am I?

Hi I'm Lilian Weng (翁荔), a PhD student in Informatics (Track in Complex Networks and Systems), School of Informatics and Computing, Indiana University Bloomington. I'm a member of the Network & Agent Networks (NaN) Research Group, Center for Complex Networks and Systems Research(CNetS). My Research interests broadly include: web mining, machine learning, social computing, social network analysis, social application, complex networks/modeling, and human computer interaction. As a PhD student, I'm closely working with Prof. Filippo Menczer, Alessandro Flammini, and Yong-Yeol Ahn on various projects related with information diffusion, social contagion, and modeling. Details on projects can be found in Projects section.
